Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Biddio LLC collects, uses, and shares information when you access our site, request beta access, or interact with our ads and analytics tools. It is designed to meet the disclosure requirements of Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, X (Twitter), and other advertising and analytics partners. If you see similar statements elsewhere in our materials, this policy controls for BIDDIO services.

Information We Collect

  • A. You Provide: Name, email, phone, credentials; contractor business details (services, service area, license/insurance); homeowner project details (job type, budget, location, photos/videos/messages); uploads (videos, notes, attachments); billing/subscription details; support communications.
  • B. Automatically: Device/browser, IP, app version/OS; usage data (pages, clicks, events); diagnostics (errors, crashes, performance); cookies and tracking data.
  • C. Third Parties: Social sign-in (if used), payment processors (subscription status, metadata), analytics/ad partners, and public/professional info you connect or import.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate BIDDIO services; match homeowners with contractors and deliver leads.
  • Enable contractor tools (lead management, CRM, crew/project, measurement workflows).
  • Process subscriptions, payments, renewals, and related notices.
  • Send transactional messages (verification, updates, support, security alerts).
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience; conduct analytics and reporting.
  • Prevent fraud/abuse, enforce terms, comply with legal obligations.
  • Send marketing communications where permitted (with opt-out controls).

How We Share Information

  • A. Between Participants: Relevant lead/project details are shared between homeowners and matched contractors.
  • B. Service Providers: Cloud/hosting, analytics/diagnostics, payments/subscriptions, communications (email/SMS/notifications), and customer support vendors processing data on our behalf.
  • C. Legal/Safety/Transfers: Disclosures required by law, to protect rights/safety, or in a merger/acquisition/financing/asset sale subject to law.
  • D. With Your Direction: When you request or consent.

Cookies, Pixels, and Tracking

  • We use cookies/SDKs to keep you signed in, remember preferences, secure sessions, measure performance, and detect fraud.
  • Advertising pixels (Meta, TikTok, X/Twitter, Google/YouTube) measure campaigns and support relevant ads; see partner controls for opt-out.
  • Browser settings and consent tools let you manage cookies; blocking some cookies may limit functionality.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Rights (where applicable): access, know, correction, deletion, portability, and opt-out of certain sharing/targeted advertising.
  • Opt out of marketing emails via unsubscribe or by contacting us.
  • We do not sell personal information for cash. Some ad-tech may be considered “sharing”; opt-out rights may apply.
  • Non-discrimination: we will not discriminate for exercising privacy rights, subject to lawful exceptions.
  • Authorized agents may submit requests where allowed by law; we may verify identity before processing.

Security, Retention, Transfers

  • Safeguards include access controls, secure transport, monitoring; no system is 100% secure.
  • We retain data as reasonably necessary for services, accounts, subscriptions, fraud prevention, disputes, and legal/tax/compliance needs, then delete or de-identify when feasible.
  • Data may be transferred internationally; protections are applied consistent with applicable law.

Additional Notices

  • Children: BIDDIO is not directed to children under 13 (or minimum age by law); we do not knowingly collect their data.
  • Third-Party Links: External sites/services are governed by their own policies.
  • Changes: We may update this policy and will post the revised “Last Updated” date; if similar statements conflict, this policy prevails.
  • Business/commercial purposes include providing services, security, fraud prevention, analytics, and improving the platform.
